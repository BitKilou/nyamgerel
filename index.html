About

A life shaped by movement.

My journey began in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia, at the age of eight, when I first stepped into the art of contortion. That early discipline laid the foundation for a career that would carry me to stages across the world.

At twenty-two, I was selected to join the Presidential Troupe of Mongolia, accompanying the President on diplomatic visits and performing for heads of state and delegations. Each appearance became a small act of cultural ambassadorship — a way to share the heritage of Mongolia through movement.

Since 2017, I have performed with Cirque du Soleil, contributing to its most celebrated productions and bringing contortion and handbalancing to audiences on every continent.
